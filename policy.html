Privacy and Policy

This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to our online activities and pertains to the information shared and/or collected on Code.iBlogger's website. It does not extend to any offline information collection or data gathered through channels other than this website.

Consent

By utilizing our website, you express your consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.

Log Files

Code.iBlogger adheres to standard procedures for log files, which are utilized to record visitor activity on websites. These log files collect data such as internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISPs), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. It is important to note that this information is not linked to any personally identifiable details. The purpose of this data is to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement on the website, and gather demographic information.

Children's Information

We are committed to safeguarding children's online experiences. We encourage parents and guardians to actively observe, participate in, and monitor their children's online activities. Code.iBlogger does not knowingly collect Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.
